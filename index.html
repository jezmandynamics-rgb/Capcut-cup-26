FAQ

Everything you'd want to ask.

Is entry free?

Yes. Entry to the CapCut Creators Cup is completely free. You only need a CapCut account and an original edit to submit.

How is the winner chosen?

A panel of CapCut editors and guest judges scores entries on creativity, technical skill, storytelling, and use of CapCut features. Community votes count toward 20% of the final score.

When is the prize paid?

Prizes are paid within 14 days of the winner announcement, after identity and originality checks are complete.

How are disputes handled?

Any dispute about scoring, eligibility, or originality is reviewed by an independent three-person panel. Their decision is final and is communicated within 7 days of the dispute being raised.
